Notes on typography, readability, and design. In spite of the fact that today's internet is more and more image, video, and multi-media-heavy by the day, one of the primary mediums of the web is still text. Good typography is an important element of design. Your typography should take into account the tone and content of what you're styling as well as the structure of that document. You can use html tags like h1, h2 (etc.) and p to structure and divide content, or divide it further with div tags. You may also need or want to create unordered lists with ul and li tags and style things like pullout quotes and links to emphasize that content. You'll also want to consider the density of the font style you choose, spacing between the lines, the width of your content, and the alignment of the text.
